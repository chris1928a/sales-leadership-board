The Core

The 6-Dimension Scoring Stack.

Each dimension maps to a proven framework with a book behind it. Not vibes.

01

Opener

Pattern interrupt or voicemail. First 30s decide everything. Chris Voss.

AnalyzedOpening line energy, hook quality, time to first question, prospect attention within 30s.
02

Pitch

SPIN-style problem framing. Not feature dumping. Rackham.

AnalyzedProblem-to-feature ratio, SPIN structure, tailoring to the prospect's segment.
03

Objection Handling My USP

LARC: Listen · Acknowledge · Reframe · Confirm.

AnalyzedResponse latency, LARC adherence, reframe precision, dodge detection, follow-up question quality.
04

Closing

Concrete next step. Date in the calendar. Oren Klaff.

AnalyzedAmbiguity in next step, date-and-time specificity, commitment obtained vs assumed.
05

Conversation Depth My USP

3 layers of "why" before pitching. Toyota 5-Whys.

AnalyzedNumber of why-probes, surface vs root-cause questioning, rep-to-prospect listening ratio.
06

Rapport

Mirror, pace, 3-second pause discipline.

AnalyzedMirroring frequency, pace match, silence discipline, tonal alignment.
Where I built my edge as a coach
Dimensions 3 and 5 are where in-person coaching hurts most. Reps learn them slowest. Managers teach them worst. That's exactly where I drilled teams for years. The system now runs that drill every single night.
